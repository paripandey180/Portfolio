My Mission

There is still inequitable representation in the field of engineering in my home country. The place I come from still requires a lot of effort to be put in towards gender equality. I hope to empower many such women who either didn’t get a chance to get quality education or couldn’t get the opportunity to pursue their dreams. I am determined to utilize my education and knowledge for self-development as well as growth of society.

Technology is used in every path of life whether it's the medical field, mechanical field, transportation or governance. There are many innovative ways possible to reach a solution. Using technology, I hope to find solutions to various problems. There is a lot of scope in the field of sustainable development. Every industry needs to be mindful of its carbon footprint. Computer technology provides a solution to this problem as well. By majoring in computer science, I will become the pioneer of technology to help mankind in achieving the objective of better living standard and in a better environment.

I do not only aim to make a career in this field but also believe to help others in learning new skills which are known to me. I am a firm believer that knowledge grows by sharing. I cannot change the whole world, but I am confident that I can give back to society in my personal capacity.
